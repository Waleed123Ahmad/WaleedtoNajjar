Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers about ordering, delivery, and the MedEqup service.

How do I place an order? +

Add the products you need to your cart, fill in your name and phone number, then click "Send Order Email". Our team will contact you to confirm stock, final price, and delivery details before dispatch.

Do prices include delivery? +

Displayed prices are for the products only. Delivery charges, if any, will be shared with you by our sales team when they confirm your order based on your location and order size.

Can I get a quotation instead of placing an order? +

Yes. Use the cart to select items and send them via the form. In your notes, mention that you need a formal quotation, and we will email you an official quote for your records.

Which areas do you serve? +

We primarily serve customers across the UAE. For large or special orders outside this area, please mention your location in the notes or contact us directly so we can advise options.
